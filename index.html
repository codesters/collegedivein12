Who we are?

Initiated by some college students, CollegeDiveIn is here to make your college life more connected, enjoyable and less frustrated. You get to meet more interesting people on your and other campuses across India.

Featured Highlights

  • Stay connected with your favourite College Clubs on your campus.
  • Explore and take part in various Events in your college.
  • Use our High Quality Notes for your college exams.
  • Work in groups on small and big projects with other people.
